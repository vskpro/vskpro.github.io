Our Quick To Make (QTM) recipes are tailor-made for your furry friend.

For information on accurate grammage, head over to Diet Calculator and input your pet’s details.
These recipes are displayed here for your reference and convenience. You can access the exact details of ingredient quantities along with the entire procedure (and a quick tip!) on the Diet Calculator page.
ADULT CANINE - WITHOUT MILK - NON VEG - BOTH ECONOMY AND PREMIUM
Ingredients:
Ingredients Quantity(grams)
Rice (raw, milled) 66.7 gram(s)
Maize (dry) 4.3 gram(s)
Soybean meal 19.5 gram(s)
French beans (country) 14.4 gram(s)
Egg (whole, boiled) 14.4 gram(s)
Chicken (skinless, boneless) 21.5 gram(s)
Chicken liver 7.2 gram(s)
Soybean oil 5.7 gram(s)
Pet diet fix NPPL mix 9.3 gram(s)

Procedure:

Soak the peas and Bengal gram overnight. Light the stove; add soya bean oil to the cooker along with the washed vegetables and mix thoroughly. Add the rice and red gram. Mix it well. Add the boiled shell-less egg, and mash it thoroughly. Use scrambled eggs if it is more convenient for you. Add two glasses of water for every glass of rice. Seal the cooker and let the mixture cook. Switch off the stove after 3 whistles. Open the cooker once it has cooled down. Add the nutritional mix to it after a gap of 5 minutes or until the food has reached room temperature. Mix well. Your recipe is ready!

Preparation time: 10 minutes.
Cooking tip:
For the busy working man and woman, some Petdietfix tips to help save time and money while cooking for your pet!
  • For quicker cooking, use pre-cooked rice and vegetables. Just light the stove, add the soybean oil. Add all the pre-cooked ingredients. Add the boiled shell-less egg, and mash it thoroughly. Use scrambled eggs if it is more convenient for you. Mix everything (so that the flavour of the yolk reaches every morsel of the rice. Your dog will absolutely love this! ) Cook the same for 3-4 minutes. Add the nutritional mix to it after a gap of 5 minutes or until the food has reached room temperature. Mix properly. Your pet’s food will be ready in less than 5 minutes. Provide the suggested quantity of milk separately.
  • Cooked vegetables have a shelf life of 4 days in the fridge. Pre-cooked vegetables and rice can make the process of cooking for your pet a much speedier process!
  • Add sufficient water separately to keep your pet hydrated!
Serving tip:
  • One table spoon is roughly 3.5 grams of petdietfix nutrition mix. You may round off the daily requirement to the nearest number of table spoons required, like 1 or 2 or 3 table spoons.
  • Uncooked rice of 1 cup generally weighs around 175-185 grams. Cooked rice of 1 cup generally weighs around 195-200 grams.
Note:
  • The recipe provided is for an entire day’s nutrition. Please divide the portions based on the number of meals you feed your pet in a day.
  • Do not add the nutritional mix while cooking, as nutrients degenerate at higher temperatures. Wait until the cooked food has reached room temperature before adding the nutritional mix. Mix well.
  • Pet Diet Fix has been tested on canines of various life stages and age groups and has proven to be 100% safe. However, some canines are especially susceptible to gassy gut. If you notice any signs of this in your canine, reduce the quantity of peas and dals by half. This will solve the problem!
