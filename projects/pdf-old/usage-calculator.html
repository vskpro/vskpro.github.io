Rice (raw, milled) 127 Gram(s)
Procedure:
Soak the peas and Bengal gram for 30 minutes before starting the cooking procedure. Light the stove; add soya bean oil to the cooker along with the washed vegetables and mix thoroughly. Add the rice and red gram. Mix it well. A pre- boiled egg can’t be mixed well in the recipe. Therefore, we suggest raw eggs. Break the raw egg in the cooker and mix the contents well (so that the flavour of the yolk reaches every morsel of the rice. Your dog will absolutely love this! ) Add two glasses of water for every glass of rice. Seal the cooker and let the mixture cook. Switch off the stove after 3 whistles. Open the cooker once it has cooled down. Add the nutritional mix to it after a gap of 5 minutes or until the food has reached room temperature. Mix well. Your recipe is ready!
Preparation time:
10 Minutes.
Cooking tip:

For the busy working men and women, some Petdietfix tips to help save time and money while cooking for your pet!

  • For quicker cooking, use pre-cooked rice and vegetables. Just light the stove, add the soybean oil. Add all the pre-cooked ingredients, break the raw egg and mix everything. Cook the same for 3-4 minutes. Add the nutritional mix to it after a gap of 5 minutes or until the food has reached room temperature. Mix well. Your pet’s food will be ready in less than 5 minutes!
  • Cooked vegetables have a shelf life of 4 days in the fridge. Proper refrigeration is needed. Pre-cooked vegetables and rice can make the process of cooking for your pet a much speedier process!
  • Add sufficient water separately to keep your pet hydrated!
NOTE:
  • The recipe provided is for an entire day’s nutrition. Please divide the portions based on the number of meals you feed your pet in a day.
  • Do not add the nutritional mix while cooking, as nutrients degenerate at higher temperatures. Wait until the cooked food has reached room temperature before adding the nutritional mix. Mix well.
