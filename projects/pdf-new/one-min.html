DIET CALCULATOR

1 MINUTE RECIPE

While QTM recipes are the best option for your pets; we at Pet Diet Fix understand the time constraints some pet parents face, while trying to keep up with their daily routines.

Yes pet parents! We have heard your call. It is for you that we have come up with our One Minute Recipe. Major components of a dog’s diet are carbs and proteins. Our Pet Diet Fix mix takes care of the rest of the micros required by your pets.

So here’s presenting our One Minute Recipe for our busybody pet parents

Adult Canine - With Milk - Non-Veg - Economy
( Full day's serving )
Ingredients Quantity(grams)
Rice (raw, milled) --
Whole cow milk --
Soybean meal --
French beans (country) --
Carrot (orange) --
Egg (whole, boiled) --
Chicken (skinless, boneless) --
Soybean oil --
Pet diet fix NA mix --

Procedure:

Clean the chicken thoroughly. Place the cooker on the stove, light it and add soybean oil to it. Add the washed chicken (anti bacterial precaution: add a quarter of a tablespoon of turmeric), corn, rice, beans, carrots and soybean meal to this. Mix it well. Add the boiled shell-less egg, and mash it thoroughly. Use scrambled eggs if it is more convenient for you. Add two glasses of water for every glass of rice. Mix everything. Seal the cooker and wait for three whistles. Open the cooker when it has cooled down.Add the nutritional mix to it after a gap of 5 minutes or until the food has reached room temperature. Mix well. Provide the suggested quantity of milk separately. Your pet’s meal is ready!

Preparation time: 10-18 minutes
Cooking tip:

For the busy working men and women, some Petdietfix tips to help save time and money while cooking for your pet!

  • For quicker cooking, use pre-cooked rice, vegetables, and par boiled chicken. Just light the stove, add the soybean oil to the cooker. Chop the required chicken into pieces. Add all the pre-cooked ingredients as well as the corn. Add the boiled shell-less egg, and mash it thoroughly. Use scrambled eggs if it is more convenient for you. Mix everything. Cook the same for 3-4 minutes. Add the nutritional mix to it after a gap of 5 minutes or until the food has reached room temperature. Mix well. Your pet’s food will be ready in less than 5 minutes! Provide suggested quantity of milk separately.

  • Cooked vegetables have a shelf life of 4 days in the fridge. Par boiled chicken has a shelf life of up to 4 days in the freezer. Pre-cooked vegetables, rice, par boiled chicken can make the process of cooking for your pet a much speedier process! Never dispose the stock solution of your chicken. It adds to the taste and has essential nutrients.

  • Add sufficient water separately to keep your pet hydrated!

Serving tip:
    • One table spoon is roughly 3.5 grams of petdietfix nutrition mix. You may round off the daily requirement to the nearest number of table spoons required, like 1 or 2 or 3 table spoons.
    • Uncooked rice of 1 cup generally weighs around 175-185 grams. Cooked rice of 1 cup generally weighs around 195-200 grams.
NOTE:
    • The recipe provided is for an entire day’s nutrition. Please divide the portions based on the number of meals you feed your pet in a day.
    • Do not add the nutritional mix while cooking, as nutrients degenerate at higher temperatures. Wait until the cooked food has reached room temperature before adding the nutritional mix. Mix well.
    • Pet Diet Fix has been tested on canines of various life stages and age groups and has proven to be 100% safe. However, some canines are especially susceptible to gassy gut. If you notice any signs of this in your canine, reduce the quantity of peas and dals by half. This will solve the problem!
