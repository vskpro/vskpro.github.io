Frequently Asked Questions


Frequently Asked Questions contains information that you can use, to make the right choices when feeding your pet. It is given in good faith, but the suitability of pet food varies for different life stage, lifestyle or health condition of the pet.

When in doubt, either write to our customer care at support@petdietfix.com or consult a veterinarian.

What is petdietfix food solution?

The pet diet fix solution combines the goodness of home food with the balance of essential vitamins, minerals and fibers, which are lost in the home food recipe. We recommend tailor-made home food recipes considering the weight, life stageand recipe categories of your pets and provide the exact nutrition mix to add to each recipe. All you have to do is mix the two and serve.

Can I use it as a nutrition supplement?

Pet diet fix is much more than a nutrition supplement. A general nutrition mix is an occasional requirement. Whereas pet diet fix mix is an essential part of the daily homefood consumed by your pets. Rest assured, with pet diet fix, your pet will get completely balanced tasty home food with entire 37 nutrients, which your pet can enjoy every day.

How do I make the pet diet fix food?

Log on to our website and choose the weight range, life stage and recipe category. You will be directed to a webpage which shows the exact daily quantity of the recipe and the right mix that will go with it perfectly. The QTM recipe making guidelines will also be available in the same page. Take a print out of it and paste it near your kitchen. Since, the website is available in 6 regional languages, it is easy to understand. Then, add the mix to your shopping cart or consult your nearest pet store/your veterinary doctor/direct retailer/pet ambassador. Then, cook the QTM recipe, add the mix as per the serving suggested (You don’t have to worry about the exact measurements. A variation of + or – 5 to 10% is perfectly fine) and a healthy, tasty, completely balanced pet home food recipe is ready.

Is the serving suggested for entire day or for 1 portion?

All pet diet fix recipe quantities are provided on the basis of daily dietary requirements. You can divide the suggested daily quantity into 2 or 3 portions depending upon the feeding schedule you already follow. When in doubt, consult your trusted veterinarian.

How does your website create the recipe?

Our unique onboard ‘usage calculator’ processes several thousands of diet calculations in a second and creates a custom recipe for your pet based on your pet’s body weight, life stage and recipe category, you chose. These variables tell the program what nutrient levels are right for your pet as well as how many calories should be provided in the daily recipe.

How long will it take to make the recipe?

Our recipes are quick to make (QTM) and can be prepared in less than 9 - 18 minutes.All ingredients of the QTM recipes were formulated after years of research by renowned scientists with decades of experience in the pet nutrition industry.

Why don't the recipes have spices and seasonings?

Many seasonings are untested on dogs and cats for safety. For example, onions and garlic are toxic to dogs and cats at levels that are well-tolerated by people. A worry would be that adding rarely used seasonings might pose a health risk.

Why are the ingredients names and amounts so specific?

We have made the pet's recipe specific because the nutrient content of the ingredients can differ significantly by cooking procedure, cut (specific to meat), inclusion of skin (such as with poultry or potatoes), etc. We have been as specific as possible in our instructions, just to ensure that your pet's diet has all the desired nutrients and intended benefits. However, a variation in quantity of 5 to 10% is perfectly fine.

What if I mix more or less of the recommended portion of Petdietfix?

Nothing to worry.We, however, advise you to follow the instruction given in our website. If you have any other doubts, you may write to us at support@petdietfix.com or consult your veterinarian.

Can I mix Pet diet fix powder with packaged pet food?

Pet diet fix mix should not be mixed with packaged pet food. On its own, pet diet fix mix has all the necessary nutrients in it. Hence, adding it to the packaged food will result in excess of nutrients and is not good for your pet. It should only be mixed with the recommended home pet food recipe, in the exact quantity suggested.

We serve 2 portions of food for our dog, every day. Can I serve packaged food as 1 portion and home food with pet diet fix mix for another portion in the same day?

Most of the pet owners in India are already feeding both packaged food and home food in a day. To elaborate, they are already feeding packaged food for 1 portion and home food for another portion in a day. This system is being followed to balance the dehydration issue, increased digestibility and also to cut down the huge costs of buying packaged pet food. You can use pet diet fix in this way too by proportionately reducing the suggested quantities of both packaged food and pet diet fix home food every day. When in doubt, kindly consult your veterinarian.

To conclude, pet diet fix food solution is already of the highest quality and will cost far lesser than the above mentioned option and it doesn’t cause dehydration and digestibility problems. Hence, for optimum results, we suggest following pet diet fix food solution only.

Can I use pet diet fix for pets with health problems?

Sure. Pet diet fix is absolutely safe and can be used without any fear for a host of health conditions such as for pets with digestive problems, pets with skin problems, pets with general weakness, pets with diabetes, pets with bone and joint problems, pets with glaucoma, pets with general sickness, slim or obese pets, for pets pre surgery, for pets post-surgery, etc. It is a general daily use pet food solution which is proven to be safe and does not have any ingredient which will cause even the slightest problem to your pet.

During summer, should I have to feed my pet only curd rice?

Pet diet fix is ideal summer food for your pet and is easy to digest. It does not cause any dehydration or heat strokes. It is a completely balanced home food for your pet and provides the entire 37 nutrients required for it. Hence it is not necessary to feed your pet curd rice alone. However, pet diet fix mix can be added to curd rice if you desire as such.

How can the Pet diet fix nutrition mix work with so many different recipes and for so many different types of dogs and cats?

Our scientists and nutrition experts have spent years refining the recipes and the nutrition mix to ensure that the result had the highest likelihood of meeting your pet's individual needs. In choosing the recipes and the nutrition mix combination, adequate care has been taken to select those ingredients which are easily available in all seasons and can be easily digestible for all life stages of pets. Pet diet fix food has been tested on thousands of canines and felines across 6 nutritional zones in India to meet the highest standards of health and safety for your pets.

Can I use pet diet fix nutrition mix with some other home food recipe that I have prepared but were not mentioned in your website?

Pet diet fix provides complete nutrition by balancing the nutrition losses of the home pet food. All the ingredients suggested in our QTM recipes are all already commonly available in your kitchen and are used by you in everyday human food preparations and pet home food preparations. Hence, they are the easiest recipes to cook and serve your beloved pet. On the other hand, you can add pet diet fix in a different home food recipe (which is already proven to be safe and healthy) and your pet will get most of the nutrients. But for optimum results, we suggest using our QTM recipes.

I forgot to add/don’t have one or two of the ingredients mentioned in the QTM recipe. Will it create any problems for my pet?

Nothing to worry. If one or the other ingredient mentioned in our QTM recipe is not available, then you can increase the quantity of other ingredients in the said recipe by 10% to attain nearest possible well balanced nutritious home meal.

Can you give us guidelines on how to choose the ingredients used in the QTM recipes?

Some common ingredients used in our QTM recipes are:

  • Chicken: Use skinless and boneless chicken
  • Eggs: Any normal egg either white or brown is O.K.
  • Milk: Use any cow milk (either skimmed or whole milk)
  • Rice: Use raw milled white rice
  • Carrot: Use orange color carrot
  • French beans: Any good quality French beans are O.K.
  • Soya bean oil: Any good quality, pure soya bean oil is O.K.
Can we use any cooking oil other than soya bean oil?

You can use any good quality cooking oil. But soya bean oil has additional benefits of soy that are highly beneficial for your pet. For optimum results, we suggest using soya bean oil.

I find it difficult to mix the exact portion of the eggs or the nutrition mix as suggested. What should I do?

One egg is roughly 60 grams. You may round off the daily requirement to the nearest number of eggs to be mixed, like 1 or 2 or 3 eggs.

One table spoon is roughly 3.5 grams of nutrition mix. You may round off the daily requirement to the nearest number of table spoons required, like 1 or 2 or 3 table spoons.

Can I mix the pet diet fix nutrition mix in water or milk instead of food?

Our nutrition mix is not soluble in water or milk, so we advise you not to mix it with either milk or water. Mix it with the QTM home food pet recipe only.

At what temperature of the QTM recipe should I mix Pet diet fix nutrition mix?

Pet diet fix nutrition mix contains vitamins and minerals which are subject to degradation at temperatures over 40 degrees C. So we advise you to mix it with the QTM recipe at room temperature

How do I introduce Petdietfix to my pet’s diet?

If you are feeding your pet totally on packaged dry food, introduce pet diet fix diet to your pets slowly in the following manner.

  • Day 1-2 = 25%
  • Day 3-4 = 50%
  • Day 5-6 = 75%
  • Day 7-8 = 100%

If you are already feeding home food, then directly introduce pet diet fix mix into the home food, from day 1.

If you are feeding one portion of packaged food and another portion of home food every day, then directly start with 50% of the suggested daily pet diet fix quantity.

I have two pet diet fix nutrition mix: one veg and one non veg. By mistake I mixed the wrong powder in the recipe, what might happen?

Do not worry. Your pet will still get most of the nutrients. We, however, advise you to follow the instruction given in our website. If you have any other doubts, you may write to us at support@petdietfix.com or consult your veterinarian.

My maid had given more than the recommended dosage of nutrient mix to my pet? What will happen tomy pet?

Nothing to worry. Our products are vigorously tested on thousands of pets and it is absolutely safe and easily digestible. A variation of 5 to 10% is ok. We, however, advise you to follow the instruction given in our website. If you have any other doubts, you may write to us at support@petdietfix.com or consult your veterinarian.

When my pet switched to the new pet diet fix home food, it developed gas, soft stools or diarrhea. What should I do?

The chances of indigestion with pet diet fix food solution are very remote. If your pet has digestion problems, it could be due to some other reason or some other food that it must have eaten.We advise you to consult your veterinarian immediately.

Can I use the left over pet meal again for my pet?

Yes. You may refrigerate it and use it again by warming it before serving, as per the serving guidelines.

What are the benefits of using pet diet fix food solution during summer?

Pet diet fix is highly beneficial to your pets especially during summers because unlike dry foods it does not cause dehydration. Your pets will find pet diet fix food much easier to digest as it has a digestibility of 90% compared to 60% digestibility of cheap quality dry packaged food. The home food nutrition mix and our pet food solutionare specially formulated to control dehydration, keeping your pet cool and happy during summer.

How many types of QTM recipes are available?

There are two types of QTM recipes: Home Food Economy and Home Food Premium. The quality, nutrition mix and essential nutrition balance are exactly the same in both the recipes.

What is the difference between QTM Home Food Economy and QTM Home Food Premium?

The QTM home food economy is intended for owners having a large number of pets. It is a highly economical alternative to breeders and shelters who maintain large number of pets.

The QTM home food premium is intended for owners having a few pets and contains all the ingredients that are commonly available in a household kitchen. Used along with the nutrition mix; it has the perfect nutrition to address the unique dietary needs of your small, medium or large pets.

Also, You can feed the best quality pet diet fix food solution to your pet at a monthly expenditure of 400-500 rupees only (including home food preparation costs) whereas it will take a minimum of 1000-1500 rupees to feed the same with packaged food diet for a whole month. Not only this breed of dog, you take any dog of any type or weight or breed, our product will be definitely 50%-70% less than the cost of the packaged food but at the same time is of highest quality. How can we categorize pets based on their activity level?

Every pet has different activity levels. At petdietfix, we recognize that the nutritional requirements of your pet must vary according to its activity level, which is why we suggest you select the appropriate level of your pet based on this information:

  • NORMAL ACTIVITY (Domestic canine): 30 minutes – 1 hour
  • SEMI ACTIVITY: 1 hour- 2 hours
  • ACTIVE: Anything above 2 hours

NOTE: The quantities we suggested are on a general basis; please refer to your veterinarian for more precise information of your pet’s activity levels.
