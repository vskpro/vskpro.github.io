Pet feeding guidelines
PET DIET FIX BEST PRACTICES FOR PET FEEDING

Every pet is unique. As a general rule you are advised to log on our website and follow the instructions given for daily recommended food intake for the ideal bodyweight of your pet

In the below guide you’ll find simple feeding guidelines based on Petdietfix diet. We advise you not to use Pet diet fix mix on any packaged wet or dry food as our mixture already contains 100% nutrition to meet your pet’s recommended daily nutrition intake.

Important points to remember:

  • Pet diet fix gives the entire daily nutrition requirement for your pets
  • If you are feeding two meals a day, you have to divide the daily amount of food in half for each meal. If you are feeding more meals, divide accordingly.
  • The 100% nutrition of pet diet fix is based on your pet having the right amount of exercise or activity. If your pet has less than 1 hour exercise or activity in a day, please lessen the amount of food by 10%
  • Your pet’s recommended daily intake of calories come 100% from QTM home food recipe + Pet diet fix nutrition mix. You may feed your pet treats or fruits such as banana, but dry food/packaged food have to be avoided/kept to a bare minimum snack serving, if you are feeding pet diet fix.

Pet Feeding Guidelines

  • Feed the pets the same food at the same time every day to maintain a regular schedule.
  • When switching to Pet diet fix, change slowly. Mix portions of both foods until you slowly phase out the old food.
    • If you are feeding your pet totally on packaged dry food, introduce pet diet fix home food diet to your pets slowly in the following manner.
    • Day 1-2 = 25% of the suggested daily serving.
    • Day 3-4 = 50% of the suggested daily serving.
    • Day 5-6 = 75% of the suggested daily serving.
    • Day 7-8 = 100% of the suggested daily serving.
    • If you are already feeding home food, then directly introduce pet diet fix mix into the home food, from day 1.
    • If you are feeding one portion of packaged food and another portion of home food every day, then directly start with 50% of the suggested daily pet diet fix quantity
  • Keep food and water bowls clean and odor free.
  • Fresh drinking water should be available to the pet at all times. Change the water regularly
  • Don't over feed your pet. Feed only the recommended quantity of the QTM recipe and the nutrition mix as suggested in our website based on your pet’s weight, life stage and recipe category.
  • Monitor your pet's weight and activity level. If it changes, enter the changed details of your pet’s age, weight or life stage categories in our website and note if any changes are to be made in the diet. Then feed your pet accordingly.
