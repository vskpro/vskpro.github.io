Feeding Instructions

(as per the pet's body weight and stomach volume)

*Feeding Interval should be 2.5hr - 3hr

For smooth transition, kindly provide 30% of the suggested daily intake on Day-1, increase to 60% on Day-2 and increase to 100% on Day-3.

Severly affected patients are recommended to start at 25% of the daily intake and increase feeding volumes according to pets acceptance.

For best results, feed liquid diet along with lukewarm water. Mild exercise is recommended.

*Refer to Aindhri Liquid Diet Guidelines
