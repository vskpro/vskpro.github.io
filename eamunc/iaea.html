International Atomic Energy Agency

The International Atomic Energy Agency is an international organisation that aims to promote the use of nuclear technology for the betterment of mankind. It does so by forming viable and effective means and methods to utilise this technology to ensure that nuclear technology doesn't cause a hindrance in future development of the world. Established on 29 July 1957 as an autonomous organisation, The IAEA with it's wide range of activities and expertise, is helping member states use nuclear techniques which provide an advantage over the conventional methods. It's main aim thus being promotion of the use of nuclear technology for establishing harmonious relations between nations there by maintaing peace around the globe

Letter from the Secretary General

Dear Delegates,

It is truly a great pleasure to welcome you all to the EA MUNC 2018. I, Ankshita Dasgupta, chair of the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA) am honored to welcome each of you to two days of team work, coordination, understanding and most importantly, diplomacy. I am proud to say, having had the opportunity of being exposed to such a dynamic environment, have positively learnt to see every situation with a different perspective.

We as the youth of our generation play an immensely important role in acting as young global citizens. In this era of sustainability, our duties as global citizens have become of utmost importance. Here at IAEA, we will be dealing with the impact of nuclear technology and how we, as a committee can formulate possible solutions to ensure that this does not become a threat in the future. The IAEA has successfully helped nations in achieving sustainability by promoting methods of nuclear technology that ensure some of our most important resources remain conserved for our future. With the future of our resources at stake, it is integral that ‘Atoms for peace and development’ take place to achieve our Sustainable Development Goals in the most efficient and practical way.

I sincerely hope that I, along with my experienced and proficient fellow bureau members - Sai Shriya Surla (Vice Chair) and Eshayath Fathima Riyas (Rapporteur) are able to give you a memorable and well deserved conference. I hope that we are able to help you and guide you in any possible manner. We hope you have wonderful conference and sincerely look forward to meeting you all.

Ankshita Dasgupta
Chair, IAEA

Topic

ATOMS FOR PEACE AND DEVELOPMENT


The International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA) promotes its Member Nations in using nuclear science and technology for harmonious, conflict - free purposes and facilitates the transfer of this technology and knowledge in an assert-able and credible manner to its Member Nations. The IAEA provides a strong and sustainable global nuclear safety and security organization, working to protect people, the society and the environment from the ionizing and harmful nuclear radiation. Through its safeguards system, the IAEA verifies that States are honoring their international agreement and understanding to use nuclear science and technology only for cordial and non – violent purposes.
